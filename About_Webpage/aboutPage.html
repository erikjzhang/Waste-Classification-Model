Our Mission

Leveraging AI to create tangible, positive environmental change by providing clear, actionable data on waste streams.

Environmental Benefits

By accurately sorting waste, we can dramatically improve recycling rates and reduce landfill volume. Our dashboard translates your efforts into measurable environmental impact based on the materials processed.

Key Impact Metrics:

Landfill Diversion:

We estimate the total weight of recyclable (plastic, metal) and compostable (organic) materials diverted from landfills, converting waste reduction into concrete numbers.

CO2 Reduction:

Recycling materials uses significantly less energy than producing them from raw resources. We calculate the estimated CO2 savings based on established industry benchmarks.

Your hackathon model will feed classification data directly into this dashboard, providing a powerful, measurable story of your project's positive impact on the environment.
