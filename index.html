The Problem

Every year, tons of recyclable and compostable materials end up in landfills, contaminating our environment. This happens because of improper waste sorting. Manual sorting is slow, expensive, and can be hazardous. This inefficiency leads to massive environmental damage and economic loss.

Our planet is facing a waste crisis. We need a smarter, faster, and more accurate way to handle our trash and ensure valuable resources are recovered, not buried.

Our Solution

This is where our project comes in. We are building an AI-powered classification system that can instantly identify different types of waste—plastic, metal, and organic matter—from an image.

By automating waste identification, we can power "smart bins" and large-scale sorting facilities, dramatically increasing recycling and composting rates. Our live dashboard visualizes this impact in real-time, showing exactly how much waste is being diverted from landfills and quantifying the positive effect on our environment.
