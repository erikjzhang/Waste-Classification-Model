Environmental Benefits

By accurately sorting waste, we can dramatically improve recycling rates and reduce landfill. Our dashboard calculates the estimated environmental impact based on the materials processed.

  • Landfill Diversion: We estimate the total weight of recyclable (plastic, metal) and compostable (organic) materials diverted from landfills.
  • CO2 Reduction: Recycling materials like plastic and metal uses significantly less energy than producing them from raw materials. We estimate the CO2 savings based on industry averages.
